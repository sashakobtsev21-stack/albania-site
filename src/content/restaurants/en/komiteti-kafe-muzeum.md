A café-bar behind the Pyramid, a short walk from Blloku, crammed wall-to-wall with communist-era bric-a-brac — radios, enamel signs and old photographs — and known for one of Albania’s widest raki selections alongside espresso and mountain tea. More a place to linger over a drink than to eat a full meal; confirm hours on arrival and find it via the map link.
